How the Programme Works

OfferUp provides 1:1 personalised mentoring, tailored to each student's goals and target divisions. Depending on the package, students receive:

  • Professional CV & application optimisation
  • Tailored networking strategy
  • Cover letter drafting & polishing
  • Interview coaching + mock interviews
  • Assessment centre preparation
  • Ongoing application support throughout the recruitment cycle
  • Flexible online sessions built around university schedules

Help you secure offers as early as possible, and build skills that last your entire career.
